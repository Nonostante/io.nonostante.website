About the game

ERMO is a relaxing puzzle game featured with a charming and peaceful graphics.

Immerse yourself in the landscapes and colors of ERMO and let you be carried away. You will learn the rules in a few seconds, but ERMO will catch you for hours.

Rearrange the tiles to get one color columns. How many blocks can you gather?

Relaxing, colorful, ERMO is a logic puzzle pleasant to play for all ages.

Do you want a break from your hectic day and just be free? Refuge yourself in the serenity of ERMO. And then you will continue to think of ERMO and be in its world, even when youâ€™ve left.

How to Play

  • Shift the tiles in horizontal and vertical
  • Make columns of one single color
  • Do not exceed the number of moves available
