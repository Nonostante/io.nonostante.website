The Game

Aedo Episodes is a logic puzzle game about the destiny of a hero you can’t control.
You have to manipulate the environment, solve enigmas and complete sidequests to allow the hero complete the journey.
The hero moves on a predefined path, you can’t change the path of the fate.

Logic and action

Use all your mental sharpness, all your logic reasoning to complete all puzzles. Have a cool head and quick reflexes to face all channges and avoid all dangers.
Only you can make the journey succeed and fulfill the destiny.

The Story

An Aedo is a storyteller and you are the audience. Our Aedo is telling you a story about humanity, about legacy, a forgotten memory faded by denial and indifference.

Please sit and listen his words of wisdom coming form a distant past.

The Curse of the Mark

Our Aedo tells the story of an unnamed hero doomed by the curse of the Mark.
People call him the Marked. The curse is a legacy from a forgotten past, a faded kingdom where people lost humanity. Back in the day, the humanity was recovered, but the mark passed through generations affecting once a while a new born.
When a Marked is born, he is the only one. The Marked has no choice. He’s raised to attend the predestined fate all of his kin must comply: preserve people humanity.

But something unexpected happened today. The curse fell upon everyone, people lost humanity again.
What happened? The Marked failed to keep people safe?
