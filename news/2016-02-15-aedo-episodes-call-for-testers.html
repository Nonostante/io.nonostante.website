Aedo Episodes - Call for testers

  • Aedo Episodes

Our first game Aedo Episodes is almost ready. The beta is open to everyone.

If you like to early access the game, wish to contribute to make a better game or you just want to evaluate and try it: you are welcome!

You can join here: Access the beta

Aedo Episodes is a puzzle adventure game where your will face tricky challenges on each level.
The game features also sidequests, minigames and a lot of secrets to be unveiled.

Each level involves two phases: the tactic phase and action phase, where you will use your logic and ability.
Lead your hero through an epic journey and overcome all dangers and challenges!
