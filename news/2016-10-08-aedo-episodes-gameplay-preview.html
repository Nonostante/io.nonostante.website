Aedo Episodes - Gameplay preview

Here’s the Aedo Episodes gameplay preview. The video focuses on the very unconventional gameplay the game features.

Hope you like it!

Aedo Episodes is a puzzle game about the fate of hero doomed by the curse of the Mark. You can’t control the hero. To let him accomplish the destiny, you have to manipulate the environment and solve tricky enigmas with logic and all your sharpness.

You can read more about Aedo Episodes and the background story in the game introduction page.

The game is scheduled to be published in the end of October.
